ABOUT
=====

Consider the problem of selecting a point within a circle (more precisely a 2-ball),
with a uniform distribution. Your inputs are any (fixed) number of random numbers.
(You may not use a discard-and-retry solution, on account of 'fixed'.)

This is a small JavaFX application exploring the square-root based solution
I ramble about at https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=19202890
